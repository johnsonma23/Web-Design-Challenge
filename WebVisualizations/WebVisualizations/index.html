Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of the weather data projects was to analyze how the weather changes the closer to the equator. To achieve this we a had to analyze dataset pulled form OpenWeatherMap API, which consisted of 500 cities.

After going through the dataset, Matplotlib was used to post various aspects of the weather vs latitude. We looked at temperature, cloudiness, wind speed and humidity. This site will provide the data we compiled and visualization created as part of the analysis, as well as explanations and descriptions of the trends and correlations witnessed.
